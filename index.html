About Me


Hello! I'm Elsie, a software engineer based in Los Angeles, California. I like building things whether it is as creative as recreating the photos I've took from my favorite sunset, as experimental as reassembling nanoparticles to produce energy, or as innovative as developing competitive websites/app. I like to create products that are truly unique and reflect their value and aesthetics.

I have a bachelor's degree in Biochemistry, with a specialized background in allergy and medical devices. I finished my master's in Biomedical Engineering and specialized in Carbon MEMS/NEMS (Micro- or Nano- Electro-Mechanical Systems), microfabrication, and electrochemical devices.

Shortly after I finished my Master's, I dabbled in programming as a hobby to build a fun game using Unreal Engine and C++. Then, this hobby led me into the field of web development where I found myself enjoying building interactive tools and intuitive web-based products.
