About Me


Hello, I'm a full-stack web developer. I create products that are truly unique and reflect their value and aesthetics.

I have a bachelor's degree in Biochemistry, with a specialized background in allergy and medical devices. I finished my master's in Biomedical Engineering and specialized in Carbon MEMS/NEMS (Micro- or Nano- Electro-Mechanical Systems), microfabrication, and electrochemical devices.

Shortly after I finished my Master's, I dabbled in programming as a hobby to build fun applications. Then, this hobby led me into the field of web development where I found myself enjoying building interactive tools and intuitive web-based products.
