I'm a full-stack web developer. I like creating products that are truly unique and reflect their value and aesthetics. I develop websites/applications that provide both intuitive UI and efficient and adaptable backends.

I have a bachelor's degree in Biochemistry and Cell Biology at University of California, San Diego. I finished my master's in Biomedical Engineering at University of California, Irvine and specialized in Carbon MEMS/NEMS and nanofibers for biosensors and electrochemical devices.

Shortly after finishing my graduate program, I dabbled in programming as a hobby to build a fun game. Then, the hobby led me into the field of web development where I found myself enjoying building fun tools for the web.
